当前线上问题分析
Current Issues Analysis
呼出不响铃：约25%的比例，符合行业标准，受网络环境、系统功耗、使用频率等多因素影响。该问题难以进一步优化，与竞品水平相当。
No-Ring Calls: Approximately 25% rate, which meets industry standards. Affected by network environment, system power consumption, usage frequency, and other factors. This issue is difficult to further optimize and is comparable to competitor levels.
接通后连接失败率：新SDK已将此问题率降至7%，后续将通过小幅度迭代持续优化。
Connection Failure Rate: New SDK has reduced this issue to 7%, with continuous optimization through minor iterations in the future.
视频流畅度问题：包括卡死、卡顿、黑屏等，6月将结合监控和用户反馈进行精准统计并优化。
Video Fluidity Issues: Including freezing, stuttering, black screens, etc. Precise statistics and optimization will be conducted in June based on monitoring and user feedback.
视频清晰度：部分用户反馈清晰度低、模糊，正结合硬件能力持续优化编码压缩算法。
Video Clarity: Some users report low clarity and blurriness. We are continuously optimizing encoding compression algorithms based on hardware capabilities.
音频质量问题：语音卡顿、回声、噪音等问题正在通过优化音频处理管道解决，6月会有更准确数据。
Audio Quality Issues: Voice stuttering, echo, noise, and other issues are being resolved through audio processing pipeline optimization. More accurate data will be available in June.
