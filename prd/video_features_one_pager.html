Video Call Enhancement Features - One Pager

ğŸªŸ Overview

This project aims to enhance VoIP video calls by introducing the following four features:

  • Background Replacement: Users can change their background before or during a video call, including blur, preset backgrounds, and custom images.
  • Emoji Avatar: Users can replace their avatar with an Emoji or virtual character for added fun.
  • AR Stickers and Accessories: Users can overlay virtual accessories and animated effects that follow their movements.
  • Beauty Filters: Users can enhance their appearance with skin smoothing and facial adjustments, along with other visual filters (black and white, retro, etc.).
  • Gift Giving: Users can send virtual gifts during calls that display special effects (fireworks, animations) visible to both parties for 5 seconds.

ğŸ›¸ Problem

  • Users want to personalize their appearance and background in video calls to improve engagement.
  • Current VoIP solutions lack flexible background replacement and real-time avatar effects.
  • Users need the ability to adjust backgrounds or apply filters in various settings (home, office) to enhance professionalism or entertainment.

ğŸ¯ Objectives

  • Allow users to replace their background before or during video calls (blur, preset, custom).
  • Enable users to change their avatar to an Emoji or virtual character.
  • Provide AR stickers and accessories that follow user movements and add interactive elements.
  • Implement beauty filters and other visual effects that enhance user appearance.
  • Create shared experiences through interactive gift-giving features with visual effects.
  • Ensure low latency and high visual quality while optimizing performance for low-end devices.

ğŸš§ Constraints

  • Only available for video calls; voice calls do not support this feature.
  • AI processing must be done on-device for privacy and low latency.
  • Algorithm optimization is required to support low-end devices with minimal performance impact.

ğŸ” Detailed Feature Analysis

Feature Description Advantages Disadvantages
Background Blur and Replacement Real-time segmentation technology separates the user from the background, allowing the background to be blurred or completely replaced with virtual images or videos. This feature helps maintain privacy and create a more professional or personalized environment.
Advantages:
  • Protects user privacy in shared spaces or work environments
  • Creates a more professional appearance for business meetings
  • Allows users to showcase hometowns or meaningful places as virtual backgrounds
  • Enhances emotional connections during long-distance communications
  • Reduces distractions from cluttered or inappropriate backgrounds
Disadvantages:
  • Edge detection may be imperfect, causing visual artifacts
  • Requires good lighting conditions for optimal segmentation
  • Higher processing requirements may affect performance on low-end devices
  • Background replacement may appear unnatural when user moves quickly
  • May cause mismatch between audio (room acoustics) and visual environment
Emoji Avatar / Virtual Character Digital avatar system that replaces the user's real appearance with a virtual character or emoji. The system captures facial expressions and movements in real-time and maps them to the virtual character, protecting privacy while maintaining natural communication.
Advantages:
  • Privacy protection - users don't need to show their real face
  • Reduces video call fatigue and self-consciousness
  • Expresses personality through customizable avatars
  • Lower bandwidth consumption compared to full video
  • Performs well in low-light environments where video quality would be poor
Disadvantages:
  • May reduce authenticity in professional settings
  • Potential "uncanny valley" effect with some avatar styles
  • Technical limitations in capturing subtle expressions
  • Might be perceived as lacking personal connection
  • Not suitable for scenarios requiring formal facial recognition
  • May not be well-received in some cultures where family calls value authentic faces
AR Stickers and Accessories Augmented reality elements that can be overlaid on the user's video. These virtual accessories, stickers, or animated effects follow the user's movements and can add fun, seasonal themes, or brand elements without completely replacing the user's face.
Advantages:
  • Adds fun and interactive elements to calls while maintaining user identity
  • Creates shared moments of joy, enhancing social connection
  • Suitable for themed calls (holidays, celebrations, special events)
  • Can incorporate brand elements for corporate use
  • Makes calls more memorable and engaging
  • Adds festive atmosphere, ideal for cross-country family interactions
Disadvantages:
  • May cause distractions in serious discussions
  • Might appear unprofessional in certain contexts
  • Processing requirements may affect device performance
  • Could divert attention from conversation content
  • Not all participants will appreciate these playful elements
Beauty Filters Real-time technology that enhances users' facial features and skin tone, smoothing skin, adjusting facial features, and enhancing lighting effects to improve personal appearance in video calls without drastically changing identity.
Advantages:
  • Enhances user confidence and comfort during video calls
  • Compensates for suboptimal lighting conditions
  • Highly customizable, from subtle to more noticeable effects
  • Relatively low computational requirements compared to other effects
  • Improves appearance while maintaining the user's true identity
  • Meets basic appearance presentation needs across different cultural backgrounds
Disadvantages:
  • Excessive use may lead to unnatural appearance
  • May reinforce social pressure regarding beauty standards
  • Different cultural backgrounds have different beauty preferences
  • Requires continuous facial tracking, may be unstable during rapid movements
  • Higher processing requirements for high-resolution video
Gift Giving Interactive feature that allows users to send virtual gifts during video calls. When activated, special effects like fireworks or animations appear on both users' screens as temporary overlays for approximately 5 seconds, creating a shared moment of celebration.
Advantages:
  • Creates interactive, celebratory moments during video calls
  • Enhances emotional connection between users with shared visual experiences
  • Provides a fun way to express appreciation during conversations
  • Temporary duration ensures effects don't disrupt the entire conversation
  • Both parties see the same effect, creating synchronized shared experiences
Disadvantages:
  • May be distracting during serious or professional conversations
  • Animation effects require additional processing power and bandwidth
  • Might not appeal to all user demographics
  • Could be overused in some contexts, leading to conversation disruption
  • Synchronization issues could affect the shared experience quality

The features will be implemented in two phases to ensure quality, stability, and to gather user feedback at each stage:

Phase 1 - Core Features

Feature Details
Background Blur Given: The user is in a video call (pre-call or in-call)
When: The user clicks the "Background" button and selects "Blur"
Then: The user's background is blurred while keeping them in focus, with adjustable intensity.
Background Replacement Given: The user has access to background blur
When: The user clicks the "Background" button and selects a preset image
Then: The user's background is replaced with the selected preset in real-time.
Basic Beauty Filters Given: The user is in a video call (pre-call or in-call)
When: The user selects the "Beauty" filter
Then: Basic skin smoothing and lighting enhancement are applied with adjustable intensity.
Visual Filters Given: The user has access to the beauty filter
When: The user selects "Visual Filters"
Then: The user can apply artistic filters (B&W, sepia, vintage, etc.) to their entire video.
AR Effects Given: The user is in a video call
When: The user selects the "AR Effects" option
Then: The user can apply basic AR effects (hats, glasses, frames) that follow their movements, as well as some simple animated effects.
Emoji Avatar Given: The user is in a video call
When: The user selects "Emoji Avatar" and chooses a character
Then: The user's video feed is replaced with an animated avatar that mimics their facial expressions.
